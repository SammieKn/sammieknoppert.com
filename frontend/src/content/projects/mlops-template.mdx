<Lead>
A repeatable starter template for taking ML prototypes from notebook to production.
</Lead>

## The Challenge

Many ML prototypes never reach production due to missing structure and checks. Data scientists build impressive demos, but the path to deployment remains unclear.

## Approach

Create a repeatable template with clear interfaces and minimal operational overhead. Focus on the 80% of cases rather than building for every edge case.

<Callout title="Philosophy">
Good defaults beat perfect flexibility. Ship something simple, then iterate.
</Callout>

## What I Built

Outlined a baseline pipeline with testing hooks and deployment-friendly packaging:

- **Project structure** – Organized folders for data, models, and pipelines
- **Testing hooks** – Unit tests for transforms, integration tests for pipelines
- **Deployment config** – Docker + CI templates ready to customize

## Results

Shorter time-to-deliver and fewer surprises when moving from demo to production. Teams can clone, customize, and ship within days instead of weeks.
